Logical operators(AND, OR, NOT) can be used with WHERE clause

- it is used to list out the rows from a table based on some condition


  AND   -  here both the conditions should be satisfied
     


  OR    - here it is enough to have atleast one condition to be satisfied



  NOT   - it will list out rows if the condtion is not true
